Anonymous – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

1. Introduction

At Anonymous, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account & Content Data: Messages, posts, or feedback you submit (stored temporarily).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data.
  • Cookies: Used to keep you logged in, remember preferences, and improve functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security risks
  • Improve features, performance, and user experience
  • Analyze traffic patterns (in anonymized form)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With service providers who help operate the platform (bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the safety, rights, or property of Anonymous and its users

5. Data Retention

We store your information only as long as necessary to provide the Service or comply with legal obligations. Temporary message data is deleted automatically after it is no longer needed.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal/security needs)
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent to data collection (where applicable)

To exercise your rights, email us at: privacy@anonymous.com.

7. Security

We use reasonable safeguards (technical, administrative, and physical) to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

8. Children’s Privacy

Anonymous is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has shared data with us, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with a revised “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the Service means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: legal@anonymous.com
